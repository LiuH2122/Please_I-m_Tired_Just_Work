About us



What is Millennium's Key Club? What do we do?

Key club is an internation service organization with thousands of clubs across the world. That's right - we don't make keys. MHS Key Club is a branch of the international organization. We give our members the opportunity to serve the community and support the causes that make the world a better place. Some of the events that we participate in include: toys shopping for kids, the blood drive, trick-or-treat for UNICEF, Feed My Starving Children, and dozens more.


Key Club aims to build leadership, compassion, kindess, and various other positive characteristics. Through volunteering, we teach members to be an active force in supporting the growth of their community, and to take action for the values they believe in. Every year, Key Club host district and international conferences. The conferences are places where students can meet and inspire other members, and understand the club and its values with more clarity.


Many colleges recognize Key Club and the values it stands for. Additionally, the organization provides various scholarship opportunities which all members can apply for.


How do I join?


If you are a student at Millennium High School, and would like to join Key Club, come visit us during club rush. If you would like to register yourself as an official member of Key Club, click on Club Section on the top right, then hit Join Club.
